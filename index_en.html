HOW TO PLAY?

Choose a card

Select a random word from the 87 available cards. There are words for all levels!

Draw the word

Use your creativity to represent the word on the canvas. It doesn't matter if you draw badly!

3

Guess and win

The other players try to guess what you have drawn. Laughter is guaranteed!
